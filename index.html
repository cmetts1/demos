MIXDIFF/CMETTS Audio Demo

In recent years, with the rapid development of deep learning technologies, neural network-based speech synthesis models have become a key technology in the field of speech synthesis. Although these models have made significant progress in the naturalness and quality of synthesized speech, they still face numerous challenges, such as high computational complexity, slow generation speed, and insufficient emotional expressiveness. To address these issues, this paper proposes two innovative speech synthesis models: MIXDIFF, based on a diffusion model, and CMETTS, based on a consistency model, aiming to improve the quality, efficiency, and emotional expressiveness of speech synthesis.
